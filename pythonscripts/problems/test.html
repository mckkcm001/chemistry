Given an average weight, find the weight of n coins in kg.
Area: ['stoichiometry']
Skill: ['number to mass conversion', 'metric conversion']
The average weight of a coin is 4.11 g. Find the total weight of 137632 coins in kg.
$$ \require{cancel} wt_{tot} = wt_{avg} \cdot n = \frac{ 4.11 \: g }{ 1 \: \cancel{coin} } \cdot 137632 \: \cancel{coin} = 565667.5 \: g = 566000 \: \cancel{g} \cdot \frac{1 \: kg}{1000 \: \cancel{g}} = 566 \: kg $$
